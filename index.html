How To Play

1. The active player gives a one-word clue related to a specific color. 2. Other players try to guess which color on the board matches that clue. 3. Points are awarded based on how close the guess is to the target color. 4. The closer your guess, the more points you earn!

Clue Examples:

  • strawberry
  • banana
  • lime
  • lavender
  • ocean
